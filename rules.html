GENERAL RULES 🕮

1. If ref column in #timetable is empty, players has to self-ref ref guide. so, not only schedule a match time (in UTC). But also need to decide who will be the referee in the match. Players can still request a ref through discord channel or dm if they willing to/can't sort it out.


2. Schedule has to be made at least 8 hours before the previous scheduled time (including who will ref in case of self-ref). Pls post the updated time and ref in the scheduling channel on discord server. For less than 8 hours, subjected by case-to-case bases.


2. If a player is not in lobby by 10 minutes after scheduled match time, they lost their bans. For 15 minutes, they ff the match.


3. warm up, you guys can sort it out yap, players will !roll to determine the pick and ban order (1st, ban 2nd pick or ban 1st pick 2nd, decided by higher roller). ABBA ban order if 2 bans, a player can't bans on the same mod pool.


4. each player has 90 seconds to pick/ban a map, both players have 90 seconds to ready up after pick. Referee can apply their common sense, if can't sort it out, dm staff ig


5. Match played in Score V2, osu standard. NoFail should be used every maps. TB are FM without the NoMod discount calculation. FM mod rules: HD/HR/HDHR as default , NM (0.85x multiplier), EZ (2.0x multiplier), EZHD (2.12x multiplier), FL and other mods combinations are not allowed


6. If player disconnected during match, It will only be vaild to compare score if they can provide a vaild proof (e.g. screenshot of score around the match map ending timestamp). Otherwise, they automatically lose the point


7. For qualifier (if any), player can make a tournament lobby (!mp make (('Player name') vs (NCT2 QL))) themself, play all the maps in one try and send the mp link to match result channel in discord. request for ref rule still apply. It should be done before 08/01/2023 23:59UTC.
